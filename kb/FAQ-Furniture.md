Category: Furniture

## English

**Q: How do I assemble the furniture?**
A: All furniture comes with illustrated assembly instructions. Video tutorials are available on our website. You can also request professional assembly service for $49-99 depending on item size.

**Q: The furniture arrived damaged. What should I do?**
A: Take photos of the damage and contact support within 48 hours. We'll send replacement parts or arrange a full exchange. Keep all packaging until the issue is resolved.

**Q: Can I return assembled furniture?**
A: Assembled furniture can only be returned if defective. For change of mind, please disassemble and repack in original packaging. A 20% restocking fee applies.

**Q: What materials are used?**
A: Product pages list all materials. Most items use solid wood, MDF, or metal frames. Upholstery options include fabric, leather, and vegan leather. Contact support for specific material questions.
