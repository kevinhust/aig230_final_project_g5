# Customer Complaints & Escalation (客户投诉与升级 / Plaintes et Escalade / Quejas y Escalada)

Category: Support
Language: en-zh-fr-es

---

## Angry Customer Detection (愤怒客户检测)

### Signs of Angry Customer:
- Using ALL CAPS
- Multiple exclamation marks (!!!) or question marks (???)
- Words like: "terrible", "worst", "scam", "lawsuit", "manager", "unacceptable"
- Repeated complaints about same issue
- Threatening negative reviews or social media posts

### Response Protocol:
1. Acknowledge frustration immediately
2. Apologize sincerely
3. Offer concrete solution or escalation
4. If unresolved after 2 exchanges, offer human transfer

## English Complaints

**Q: This is UNACCEPTABLE! I've been waiting 2 weeks for my order!!!**
A: I sincerely apologize for this frustrating delay. Let me immediately check your order status and escalate this to our shipping team. Please provide your order number and I'll personally ensure this is resolved today.

**Q: Worst customer service ever! I want to speak to a manager NOW!**
A: I'm truly sorry you've had such a poor experience. Your feedback is important. I'm escalating your case to a supervisor who will contact you within 2 hours. In the meantime, how can I help resolve your immediate concern?

**Q: You guys are a SCAM! I'm reporting to BBB and posting on social media!**
A: I understand your extreme frustration and want to make this right immediately. Please provide your order details - I'll personally investigate and ensure you receive either your product or a full refund today. We take this very seriously.

## Escalation Script (升级话术)

When transferring to human:

- ZH: "我将为您转接高级客服团队，他们能更好地处理您的问题。请稍等。"
- FR: "Je vous connecte à notre équipe senior qui pourra mieux vous aider. Veuillez patienter."
