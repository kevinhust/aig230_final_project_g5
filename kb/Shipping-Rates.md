# Shipping Rates & Delivery (运费与配送 / Tarifs de Livraison / Tarifas de Envío)

Category: Policies
Language: en-zh-fr-es

---

## English

**Q: Do you offer free shipping?**
A: Yes! Free standard shipping on orders over $50. Express shipping is available for $9.99. Same-day delivery available in select cities for $14.99.

**Q: How long does standard shipping take?**
A: Standard shipping: 5-7 business days. Express: 2-3 business days. Same-day: Order by 2 PM for delivery by 9 PM.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: We ship to Canada, USA, UK, EU, Australia, and most Asian countries. International shipping rates vary by weight and destination.

**Q: Can I track my order?**
A: Yes, tracking numbers are sent via email within 24 hours of shipment. You can also track in your account under "Order History."
