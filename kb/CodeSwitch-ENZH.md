## Common Mixed Language Queries (常见混合语言查询)

### Electronics (电子产品)

**Q: 我的phone screen cracked，能修吗？warranty cover吗？**
A: Cracked screens from accidental damage are not covered by standard warranty. However, we offer repair services starting at $99. You may also have coverage if you purchased extended accident protection.
屏幕意外损坏不在标准保修范围内。但我们提供$99起的维修服务。如果您购买了延保意外保护，可能可以获得覆盖。

**Q: Laptop battery die很快，能replace吗？under warranty?**
A: Yes, battery degradation within 1 year is covered by warranty. Please contact support with your order number to arrange replacement.
是的，1年内的电池衰减在保修范围内。请联系客服并提供订单号安排更换。

**Q: 这个tablet很lag，可以return吗？**
A: You can return within 30 days if the device has performance issues. Please contact support for troubleshooting first - we may be able to resolve the issue without return.
30天内如果设备有性能问题可以退货。请先联系客服进行故障排除 - 我们可能无需退货即可解决问题。

### Appliances (电器)

**Q: Fridge噪音太大，很annoying，怎么搞？**
A: First, check if the refrigerator is level. Also ensure nothing is touching the back wall. If noise persists, contact support - it may be a compressor issue covered by warranty.
首先检查冰箱是否放平。也要确保没有物品接触后壁。如果噪音持续，联系客服 - 可能是保修范围内的压缩机问题。

**Q: Washer漏水了，floor都wet了，emergency！**
A: Turn off the washer and unplug immediately. Check if the drain hose is properly connected. Contact support for emergency service - water damage may be covered by warranty.
立即关闭洗衣机并拔掉电源。检查排水管是否正确连接。联系客服进行紧急服务 - 水损可能在保修范围内。

**Q: Microwave不heat了，怎么办？**
A: Try a different outlet first. If still not heating, the magnetron may need replacement - this is covered under warranty. Contact support to arrange repair.
先尝试不同的插座。如果仍不加热，可能需要更换磁控管 - 这在保修范围内。联系客服安排维修。

### Shipping & Returns (配送与退货)

**Q: Order还没到，tracking不动了，what happened?**
A: Tracking may take 24-48 hours to update. If no movement after 48 hours, contact support with your order number - we'll investigate with the carrier.
追踪信息可能需要24-48小时更新。如果48小时后仍无动态，请提供订单号联系客服 - 我们将与承运商调查。

**Q: 收到的item damaged了，想return，怎么弄？**
A: Take photos of the damage immediately and contact support within 48 hours. We'll arrange return shipping and send a replacement or refund.
立即拍摄损坏照片并在48小时内联系客服。我们将安排退货运输并发送替换品或退款。

**Q: Size不对，想exchange，但是out of stock了**
A: We can notify you when the item is back in stock, or offer alternatives. You can also choose a refund and repurchase later.
我们可以通知您补货，或提供替代品。您也可以选择退款并稍后重新购买。

### Payment & Coupons (支付与优惠)

**Q: Payment failed，钱扣了但是order没成功**
A: Failed payments usually result in automatic refunds within 3-5 business days. If you don't see the refund, contact support with your payment confirmation.
支付失败通常会在3-5个工作日内自动退款。如果未看到退款，请提供支付确认联系客服。

**Q: 有student discount吗？我是学生**
A: Yes! Students get 15% off with valid .edu email verification. Contact support to verify your student status.
有！学生用.edu邮箱验证可享85折。联系客服验证您的学生身份。

**Q: Coupon code不work，why？**
A: Check if the code is expired or if minimum purchase isn't met. Some codes exclude sale items. Contact support if you believe there's an error.
检查代码是否过期或未达最低消费。有些代码排除打折商品。如果您认为有误，请联系客服。
