# Smart Home Devices (智能家居 / Appareils Domotiques / Dispositivos Inteligentes)

Category: Electronics
Language: en-zh-fr-es

---

## English

**Q: How do I connect my smart device to Wi-Fi?**
A: 1) Download our app from App Store or Google Play. 2) Create an account. 3) Tap "Add Device" and follow on-screen instructions. 4) Make sure your phone is on 2.4GHz network (not 5GHz).

**Q: My smart speaker isn't responding to voice commands.**
A: 1) Check if the microphone is muted (look for red light). 2) Ensure Wi-Fi connection is stable. 3) Restart the device by unplugging for 10 seconds. 4) Re-link in the app if needed.

**Q: Can I control devices when I'm away from home?**
A: Yes! As long as your device is connected to Wi-Fi and your phone has internet, you can control from anywhere. Enable "Remote Control" in app settings.

**Q: How do I share device access with family members?**
A: In the app, go to Device Settings > Share Access. Enter family member's email or phone number. They'll need to create an account to accept.

## 中文 (Chinese)

**Q: 如何将智能设备连接到Wi-Fi？**
A: 1) 从App Store或Google Play下载我们的应用。2) 创建账户。3) 点击"添加设备"并按屏幕提示操作。4) 确保手机连接2.4GHz网络（不是5GHz）。

**Q: 智能音箱不响应语音命令。**
A: 1) 检查麦克风是否静音（查看是否有红灯）。2) 确保Wi-Fi连接稳定。3) 拔掉电源10秒后重启。4) 如需要，在应用中重新链接。

**Q: 离家时可以控制设备吗？**
A: 可以！只要设备连接Wi-Fi且手机有网络，您可以在任何地方控制。在应用设置中启用"远程控制"。

**Q: 如何与家人共享设备访问权限？**
A: 在应用中，进入设备设置 > 共享访问。输入家人的邮箱或电话号码。他们需要创建账户来接受邀请。
