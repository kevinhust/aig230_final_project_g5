## Refrigerator & Freezer Issues (冰箱冰柜问题)

### English
**Q: My refrigerator is making loud noises. Is this normal?**
A: Some humming is normal (compressor). Loud or unusual noises may indicate: 1) Not level - adjust feet. 2) Items touching back wall. 3) Condenser coils need cleaning. 4) Compressor issue (warranty covered).

**Q: Water dispenser not working.**
A: 1) Check water supply is connected. 2) Replace water filter (every 6 months). 3) Check for frozen water line. 4) Ensure dispenser lock is off.

**Q: Ice maker not producing ice.**
A: 1) Ensure ice maker is turned on. 2) Check water supply. 3) Clear any jammed ice. 4) Wait 24 hours after installation for first ice.

### 中文
**Q: 冰箱噪音大，正常吗？**
A: 轻微嗡嗡声正常（压缩机）。大声或异常噪音可能因为：1) 不平整 - 调节脚垫。2) 物品接触后壁。3) 冷凝器线圈需清洁。4) 压缩机问题（保修范围内）。

**Q: 出水口不工作。**
A: 1) 检查水源是否连接。2) 更换滤水器（每6个月）。3) 检查水管是否冻结。4) 确认出水口锁已关闭。

## Washing Machine Issues (洗衣机问题)

### English
**Q: Washer won't drain.**
A: 1) Check for clogged drain filter. 2) Ensure drain hose isn't kinked. 3) Don't overload. 4) Check drain pump (may need replacement).

**Q: Washer is shaking/vibrating excessively.**
A: 1) Ensure machine is level. 2) Don't overload. 3) Remove shipping bolts (new installations). 4) Check for item stuck between drum and tub.

**Q: Clothes come out wet after spin cycle.**
A: 1) Reduce load size. 2) Select higher spin speed. 3) Check drain hose for blockage. 4) Drain pump may be failing.

### Français
**Q: Le lave-linge ne vidange pas.**
A: 1) Vérifiez le filtre de vidange. 2) Assurez-vous que le tuyau n'est pas plié. 3) Ne surchargez pas. 4) Pompe de vidange peut-être défectueuse.

## Dryer Issues (烘干机问题)

### Español
**Q: La secadora no calienta.**
A: 1) Verifica el fusible térmico. 2) Limpia el filtro de pelusa. 3) Verifica la ventilación. 4) Elemento calefactor puede estar dañado.

**Q: La secadora tarda demasiado.**

## Code-Switching Examples

**Q: 我的washer不drain，怎么fix？**
A: Check drain filter for clogs first. 先检查排水过滤器是否堵塞。Also ensure hose isn't kinked. 也要确认水管没有弯曲。

**Q: Refrigerator hace ruido, es normal?**
A: Light humming is normal compressor sound. 轻微嗡嗡声是正常的压缩机声音。Loud noises may need service. 大噪音可能需要维修。

**Q: Dryer no calienta, warranty cubre?**
A: Yes, heating element is covered under 1-year warranty. 是的，加热元件在1年保修范围内。Contact support for repair. 联系客服安排维修。
